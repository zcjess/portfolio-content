Landscape

In 2019 May, I went to Hawaii with my mom for two weeks. We stayed in the island Ouhu for about one week and saw lots of beautiful scenes.

We went to a lighthouse to see sunrise at 5am, which is such a great experience. There are people waiting for the sunrise on the rock, which constitutes an appealing composition.
